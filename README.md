# 🌍 Global Retail Sales Analysis  

> **Interactive Data Analytics Project** built with **SQL**, **Claude AI**, and **Power BI** to analyze global sales trends of a retail chain dealing in office and stationery items.  

---

## ✨ Overview  
This project provides a comprehensive **Global Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard** using **Power BI**.  
- 📊 SQL queries were written to extract key business insights.  
- 🤖 Claude AI was used for **query analysis & insight generation**.  
- 🖥️ Power BI dashboards visualize **KPIs, market analysis, product performance, and customer behavior**.

## 📈 Analysis  

### 🏆 Which products have the best profit margin by subcategory?  
- Several **Office Supplies** subcategories hit the maximum **50% profit margin**.  
- High-margin products include **Art, Paper, Labels, Fasteners, and Accessories**.  

|Category       |Subcategory |Product                              |Profit Margin (%)|
|---------------|------------|-------------------------------------|-----------------|
|Furniture      |Chairs      |SAFCO Steel Folding Chair            |41.94            |
|Office Supplies|Paper       |Xerox 1890                           |50.00            |
|Technology     |Accessories |Belkin Memory Card                   |50.00            |

---

### 👥 Which customers spend the most by segment?  
- **USA** dominates with **top spenders in all 3 segments**.  
- **India** has top customers across multiple categories.  
- **Tom Ashbrook (Home Office, US)** tops all with **$13,716 sales**.  

---

### 📦 Are the top products by sales the same as the top by profit?  
- ✅ **Technology & Office Supplies** → Strong overlap.  
- ❌ **Furniture** → No overlap, showing sales ≠ profit correlation.  

---

### 🌆 Are the top cities by sales the same as the top by profit?  
- **USCA & Africa** → High similarity.  
- **Asia Pacific** → No overlap at all.  
- 📊 Only **40% of cities** appear in both rankings.  

---

## 🚀 Conclusion  
🔹 High sales ≠ high profits (especially in **Furniture**).  
🔹 Market dynamics vary significantly across **regions & categories**.  
🔹 **US & India** are critical markets across all segments.
